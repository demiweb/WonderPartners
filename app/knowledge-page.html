Winter wheat is one of the most valuable cereals in the world. However, today farmers do not always get stable yields, and a significant proportion of grain does not meet food conditions. There has been a decrease in the protein content of grain recently, as well as the deterioration of other technological indicators.

Fertilizer system organization plays significant role when it comes to obtaining high yields of quality grain of wheat, barley and other cereals. Analysis of grain yields in Ukraine shows that they have significant potential to increase and improve grain quality. However, nowadays mineral nutrition with only the main macronutrients N:P:K is not enough for this. It is necessary to provide the plant with micro elements. Foliar fertilization of crops can be an effective agronomic measure in providing plants with micronutrients during the growing season. They are especially effective in arid conditions of the steppe zone since they increase availability of nutrients and stimulate their assimilation by plants from the soil.
